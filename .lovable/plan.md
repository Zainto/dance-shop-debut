

## Dance Shop Landing Page

### Pages & Sections

**Landing Page (single page)**

1. **Hero Section** — Full-width banner with a bold headline like "Move with Grace, Shop with Style", a subheadline about the shop, and a "Shop Now" CTA button. Background with a warm, elegant gradient or dance-themed imagery placeholder.

2. **Featured Categories** — Grid of 4 product category cards with icons and labels: Ballet, Jazz, Contemporary, and Hip Hop. Each card has a hover effect and links nowhere for now (placeholder).

3. **Best Sellers** — Horizontal row of 4 product cards showing dance shoes/apparel with image placeholders, product name, price, and an "Add to Cart" button (non-functional, visual only).

4. **About / Why Choose Us** — Three-column section with icons highlighting: Free Shipping, Expert Fitting Advice, and Easy Returns.

5. **Newsletter Signup** — Simple email input with a "Subscribe" button and a short tagline.

6. **Footer** — Logo, navigation links, social media icons, and copyright.

### Design Style
- Elegant and modern with soft pinks, warm golds, and dark charcoal accents
- Clean typography with a mix of serif headings and sans-serif body text
- Smooth hover animations on cards and buttons
- Fully responsive (mobile-friendly)
